TASTES OF INDIA

Hyderabad – It’s not just about the Biryani

In the gastronomic haven of Hyderabad, while the world sings praises for its legendary biryani, the city's culinary landscape unfolds a diverse symphony of flavors that extends far beyond this aromatic delight. Beyond the tantalizing biryani plates, Hyderabad beckons food enthusiasts with a myriad of culinary treasures. From the bustling street food scene near Charminar, where kebabs and Haleem reign supreme, to the delectable Nizami sweets of Laad Bazaar, the city embraces a rich tapestry of tastes. Each bite is a journey through the intricate spices and unique recipes that define Hyderabad's culinary identity. So, while biryani remains an undisputed star, the city's food scene is a delightful expedition that promises a feast for the senses and a culinary adventure beyond the world-famous biryani.

Indore – The Street Food Capital of India

Nestled in the heart of India, Indore proudly wears the title of the Street Food Capital with unmatched flair. Beyond its historical charm, this city is a culinary haven where every street corner unfolds a feast for the senses. Indore's street food is a celebration of flavors, from the spicy delights of poha and jalebi at Sarafa Bazaar to the savory goodness of aloo kachori at Chappan Dukaan. The aroma of garadu (fried yam) and the sizzle of johnny hot dogs create an irresistible symphony for food enthusiasts. Indore's street vendors are maestros crafting culinary masterpieces, ensuring that every bite tells a story of tradition, innovation, and a unique local palette. So, when in Indore, immerse yourself in the city's vibrant streets, savor the delectable chaats, and let the Street Food Capital of India enchant you with its gastronomic symphony.

Goa – Sun, Sand and (awesome) Sea food

In the tropical paradise of Goa, the trifecta of sun, sand, and awesome seafood creates a culinary symphony that echoes the laid-back coastal vibe. Beyond its picturesque beaches, Goa is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a seafood extravaganza. Shack-lined shores serve up a palate-pleasing array of dishes, from the succulent prawn curry and fish thali to the spicy vindaloo that tingles the taste buds. Freshly caught treasures from the Arabian Sea transform into culinary delights, captivating the senses with every flavorful bite. Whether it's the beachside ambiance of Palolem or the vibrant markets of Mapusa, Goa's seafood offerings are a testament to the region's coastal charm and culinary prowess. So, in Goa, let the waves serenade you while the seafood feast transports you to a gastronomic paradise.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Lucknow – Cuisine of the Nawabs of Awadh

In the heart of Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow, the city of nawabs, unfolds a culinary tale that resonates with opulence and refinement. The cuisine of the Nawabs of Awadh is a symphony of flavors, where every dish is a carefully crafted masterpiece. From the iconic kebabs, notably the succulent galouti and aromatic seekh, to the melt-in-the-mouth goodness of Lucknawi biryani, each culinary creation reflects a rich culinary heritage. The Tunday Kababi and the bustling lanes of Aminabad encapsulate the essence of this royal cuisine. The use of fragrant spices, slow-cooking techniques, and an emphasis on artistic presentation elevate Awadhi cuisine to an unparalleled culinary experience. So, in Lucknow, let the culinary legacy of the nawabs unfold as you savor every bite, and allow the city's gastronomic prowess to transport you back to the grandeur of the Awadhi era.
